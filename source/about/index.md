## Welcome to China Travel Guide

We help foreign travelers plan unforgettable trips to China. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, our guides provide practical, up-to-date information to make your journey smooth and enjoyable.

## What We Offer

- **Visa Guides**: Step-by-step application instructions
- **City Itineraries**: Curated plans for major destinations
- **Transportation**: How to get around like a local
- **Cultural Tips**: Navigate China with confidence
- **Practical Advice**: From SIM cards to mobile payments

## Our Mission

To make China accessible to everyone. We believe travel bridges cultures and creates understanding. Our goal is to help you experience the real China, beyond the tourist traps.
